Meet Lama Ahmad

My name is Lama, founder of LAMA ART. I'm a fine artist, illustrator based in Saudi arbia.

My work is defined by sensitive brush or charcoal strokes, while the live,emotional and love is a fundamental inspiration.

Ever since I was a little girl, I’ve been drawing every chance I can get (even all over the attic staircase).

Back then, I already knew I wanted to do something creative.

In my online shop, you can buy phone casess ,note book ,original paintings and art prints.

If I'm not working in my atelier, you can find me snuggled up in my home or garden with my family.
